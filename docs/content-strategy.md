# Content Strategy

## Primary content goal

The content of `camilleaubert.com` must position Camille Aubert as:
- an AI Solutions Architect,
- a high-level technical professional,
- a credible and modern expert,
- a person combining architecture, delivery, and product sensitivity.

## Language
- V1 is English-only.

## Tone
The tone must be:
- professional,
- human,
- credible,
- sharp,
- not overly corporate,
- not AI-generic,
- not exaggerated.

## Content priorities for V1
- Hero statement
- Short positioning summary
- About section
- Experience highlights
- Selected project mentions
- Training section
- Stack section
- Education section
- Contact section

## Content constraints
- Keep it concise.
- Keep it premium.
- Avoid jargon overload.
- Avoid junior-sounding technical lists.
- Avoid fake thought-leadership language.

## Source materials
Content will be produced from:
- Camille Aubert’s real experience,
- LinkedIn content,
- internal positioning work,
- LLM-assisted drafting and rewriting.
